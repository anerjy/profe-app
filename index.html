Profe: Handwriting Tutor - Support

Profe is a handwriting tutor that runs entirely on your iPhone: write a question, snap a photo of homework, or ask by voice, and Profe solves it step by step - offline, with no account and no data collection.

Frequently asked

  • Does Profe need the internet? No. The AI model ships inside the app. The optional Search mode is the only feature that goes online, and it is off by default.
  • Where is my data? On your device only. See the Privacy Policy.
  • How do I clear the conversation? Tap "Clear conversation" under the writing board.
  • Languages? English, Spanish, and Chinese - tap the globe button to switch.
